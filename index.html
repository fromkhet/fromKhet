Know More!

To आत्मनिर्भर भारत and beyond!

This is a chance for you and us, to bring a disruptive change in the lives of our farmers.

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought our world to its knees. As many countries are trying to revive their economies with stimulus packages, our country has espoused the idea of self reliance. This is a chance for us to go back to our roots, and what better way of doing so than going back to our literal roots - the farms and our farming community?

  • Agriculture, with its allied sectors, is the largest source of livelihoods in India
  • 70% of our rural households still depend primarily on agriculture for their livelihood, with 82% of farmers being small & marginal
  • Yet, the pace & rate of disruptive technological development in this space has been dismal

The result? The most important sector in our society has remained ignored, for decades...

  • Farmers are left frustrated because of the fractured supply chains
  • They are left dejected because of an insipid & inefficient support system
  • And most are sinking deep into poverty because of exploitative practices by large corporates & bureaucrats

They repeatedly face the same set of problems - crop failure, debt burden, crippled government policies, corruption in subsidies, which sometimes even pushes them to their suicides. And this forced us to start searching for solutions.

We are a bunch of crazy nerds who are sick of the state that our farming community continues to be in. We are a group of people who want to bring that change, with your help.

What do we plan to do?

The long term vision that we have set before ourselves is to become a fully integrated agri-tech company. And with fromKhet, we have taken that first step.

Farmer Onboarding

We have onboarded farmers onto our platform to bring fresh vegetables & fruits directly to you, straight from their farms

Lean Logistics

We have cut out the middle logistics, which helps us deliver those products directly to you, as fresh as they were on the farms

Maximizing Farmer Earnings

All of this helps us ensure that the farmers get a significantly higher rate for their products as compared to conventional methods

Try us, to deliver your vegetables & fruits

Being able to help our farming community is what made us start fromKhet. And being able to serve you, is a privilege we would like to have.
